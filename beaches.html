Iceland's Black Sand Beaches

The black sand beaches of Iceland are considered to be extremely rare, as these natural attractions are quite uncommon to see on Earth. The most famous of these beaches, Reynisfjara, is found on the southern part of Iceland, and is home to many tourists every day. In fact, National Geographic once named it a part of their top ten beaches to visit in the world. These beaches were also seen in the popular TV show, Game of Thrones. The sand itself is black due to the basalt lava that covers the beach, and as a result we can see numerous basalt columns. However, as nice as this attraction is, it is also one of Iceland's most dangerous attractions, as the powerful waves of the beach have taken individuals away numerous times over the years.
